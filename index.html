My Story

I started my creative journey back in 2016 — when I first opened Photoshop while studying IT at the Islamic University. What began as curiosity, soon became a mission to visualize stories that connect and inspire.

In 2018, I shifted my academic path to Multimedia at UCAS — diving deep into design, motion graphics, and Blender.

Today, I am an Art Director & Motion Designer with a passion for building brands, leading visual teams, and creating visuals that leave an impact.
